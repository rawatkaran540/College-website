Dr. K.N.S. Kang

Dr. K.N.S. Kang, Chairman cum Director General, PCTE Group of
Institutes, Ludhiana completed his Electrical Engineering from
Regional Engineering College, Kurukshetra in 1986 and then did his
MBA & Ph.D in Business Management. He stood fourth in State in
Higher Secondary Examination and has been National Level Athlete
and won Inter University competition in cultural activities and for his all
round performance; he received Shaheed Bhagat Singh State Youth
Award.
He also got best alumni award from NIT Kurukshetra & Department of
Business Management, PAU Ludhiana. He got education evangelist
2015 award on Times Now. Dr Kang also held top positions in state
level Association and Projects.
